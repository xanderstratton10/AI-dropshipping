Refund Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. 30-Day Money Back Guarantee

We are confident in the quality of CleanSpin. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

2. Returns Process

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@cleanspin.co with your order number. We will provide you with return instructions and a return shipping address.

3. Condition of Returns

Items must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as received. We reserve the right to deny refunds for items that show excessive wear or damage.

4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

5. Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable. Customers are responsible for paying the return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or defective.
