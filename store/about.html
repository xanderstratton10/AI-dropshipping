The CleanSpin Story

CleanSpin was born out of frustration. Our founders, a team of busy parents and professionals, realized that the most tiring part of home maintenance was the manual labor involved in deep cleaning. We spent hours scrubbing bathtubs, kitchen tiles, and grout lines, only to end up with sore backs and raw hands.

We knew there had to be a better way. We set out to create a tool that combines high-torque motor technology with ergonomic design—something powerful enough for professional results, but light enough for anyone to use.

After months of prototyping and testing, CleanSpin™ was launched. Since then, we've helped over 12,000 customers take back their time and enjoy a cleaner, healthier home without the effort.
