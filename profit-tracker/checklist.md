# Weekly Review Checklist (Monday Morning)

This checklist should be completed every Monday morning to ensure the CleanSpin store is performing optimally and to plan for the upcoming week.

## 1. Ad Performance Review
- [ ] **Check ROAS:** Review Return on Ad Spend for all active campaigns on TikTok/Facebook. Target is ≥ 2.5x.
- [ ] **Review CTR & CPC:** Analyze Click-Through Rate (target > 1%) and Cost Per Click.
- [ ] **Action:** Kill any ad sets that have spent >1.5x AOV without a sale, or are below 2.0x ROAS for 3 consecutive days.

## 2. Profit Margin Analysis
- [ ] **Calculate Net Margin:** Use the Profit Tracker to calculate actual net margins for the previous week.
- [ ] **Monitor COGS:** Check for any price increases from the supplier.
- [ ] **Target:** Maintain a net profit margin of 35%+.

## 3. Inventory & Sourcing
- [ ] **Inventory Levels:** Verify current stock with supplier or 3PL.
- [ ] **Reorder Point:** If current stock is less than 14 days of average sales, place a new order today.
- [ ] **Lead Times:** Confirm current shipping lead times from China to avoid fulfillment delays.

## 4. Customer Satisfaction
- [ ] **Review Feedback:** Read all customer reviews from the past week.
- [ ] **Email Inquiries:** Ensure all support tickets are responded to within 24 hours.
- [ ] **Shipping Speed:** Check tracking numbers to ensure "7-12 day" delivery promise is being met.

## 5. Operations & Content
- [ ] **Content Calendar:** Plan next week's TikTok/Instagram posting schedule.
- [ ] **Automation Sync:** Brief Automation Specialist on any adjustments to email flows or upsell sequences.
- [ ] **Competitor Check:** Spend 15 minutes scanning competitor ads and pricing.
